## Current Research

### GNN-Based Detection of Disjunctive Structures in MILP
**OR Lab, Nanjing University** | *Apr 2025 -- Present*

Investigating how Graph Neural Networks can identify indicator-variable disjunctions in Mixed-Integer Linear Programs during preprocessing to strengthen LP relaxations.

**Key Focus:**
- Graph representation learning for MILP instances
- Variable-constraint correlation modeling
- Improved branching heuristics for MILP solvers

**Tools:** Python, PyTorch, Gurobi
